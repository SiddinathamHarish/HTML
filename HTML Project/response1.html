Paneer Tikka
Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection, often served with mint chutney. A popular appetizer at gatherings.

Aloo Gobi
A dry curry made with potatoes and cauliflower, seasoned with turmeric and other spices. A staple in North Indian households.

Dal Makhani
creamy lentil dish made from black lentils and kidney beans, slow-cooked with butter and cream. Best enjoyed with naan or rice.

Palak Paneer
Spinach puree cooked with paneer cubes and spices, offering a nutritious and flavorful curry.

Chana Masala
Chickpeas simmered in a spicy tomato-based sauce, often served with rice or flatbreads.

Vegetable Biryani
A fragrant rice dish cooked with mixed vegetables and aromatic spices, garnished with fried onions.

Dosa with Sambar
A crispy South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, served with a lentil-based vegetable stew called sambar.

Baingan Bharta
Roasted and mashed eggplant cooked with onions, tomatoes, and spices, delivering a smoky flavor.

Rajma Chawal
Kidney beans cooked in a spiced tomato gravy, served with steamed rice—a comfort food favorite.

Jeera Aloo
Potatoes sautéed with cumin seeds and spices, often enjoyed as a side dish with Indian breads.


Non vegitarian section

Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani)
A creamy and rich chicken dish cooked in a buttery tomato sauce, originating from Delhi.
Chicken Biryani
Fragrant basmati rice cooked with marinated chicken and a blend of spices, often served with raita.
Tandoori Chicken
Chicken marinated in yogurt and seasoned with tandoori masala, cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) for a smoky flavor.
Fish Curry
Varieties of fish cooked in a spicy and tangy curry sauce, popular in coastal regions.
Rogan Josh
A flavorful curry dish of tender lamb cooked with yogurt, garlic, and aromatic spices like cloves, bay leaves, and cardamom.
Chicken Tikka Masala
Grilled chicken in a creamy tomato-based sauce with spices.
Hyderabadi Dum Biryani
A slow-cooked biryani dish with tender mutton or chicken, layered with fragrant rice, saffron, and spices.
Mutton Rogan Josh
Similar to the lamb version but made with mutton, known for its rich flavors and tender meat.

Paya Soup – Bhopal
A healthy lamb dish that uses ground spices, onion, turmeric, chana dal, and grated coconut.
Haleem – Hyderabad
A delicious stew of meat, lentils, and wheat, widely celebrated during Ramadan month.
Tunde Kebab – Lucknow
This melt-in-the-mouth dish is a part of Awadhi cuisine, made with minced meat, rose water, spices, kewra, and saffron strands.
Bheja Masala – Mumbai
Also known as Bheja Fry, this side dish is made with goat’s brain seasoned with spices and then pan-fried on slow heat.
Chicken Chettinad – Tamil Nadu
One of the most popular dishes that uses onion, curry leaves, tomatoes, curd, coconut, and poppy seeds.
Kankada Jhola – Odisha
A crab curry that uses cinnamon, garam masala, and other ground spices, best served with rice.
Poora Haah – Assam
Roasted duck from Assamese cuisine, which also uses eggs, onions, tomatoes, and ground spices.

Pizza section

Margherita Pizza
A Neapolitan classic featuring tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and basil, representing the colors of the Italian flag.

Pizza Marinara
One of the oldest styles, topped with tomato sauce, garlic, oregano, and olive oil, without cheese.
Pizza Quattro Formaggi
A rich blend of four cheeses—typically mozzarella, Gorgonzola, fontina, and Parmesan—melted together for a creamy delight.
Pizza Quattro Stagioni
Divided into four sections, each representing a season with toppings like artichokes (spring), tomatoes and basil (summer), mushrooms (autumn), and prosciutto with olives (winter).
